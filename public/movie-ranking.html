Juston's Top Movie List

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Seven pounds

"Seven Pounds" is a drama film that explores themes of redemption and sacrifice, starring Will Smith as a man with a mysterious past who seeks to atone for his actions by changing the lives of seven strangers in profound ways.

Step Brothers

"Step Brothers" is a comedy film that follows the hilarious and chaotic journey of two middle-aged stepbrothers, played by Will Ferrell and John C. Reilly, as they struggle to coexist and eventually form an unexpected bond..

Scarface

"Scarface" is a classic crime film directed by Brian De Palma, depicting the rise and fall of Tony Montana, played by Al Pacino, as he transforms from a Cuban immigrant into a ruthless and powerful drug lord in 1980s Miami.
